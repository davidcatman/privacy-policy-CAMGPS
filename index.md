    <h2>1. Who we are</h2>
    <p>This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application <strong>CamGPS</strong>, published by <strong>CATMAN STUDIO</strong> (“we”, “us”, “our”).</p>

    <h2>2. Summary</h2>
    <p>CamGPS is a camera app that can stamp GPS and notes on photos you take. The optional <strong>photo map</strong> shows where your CamGPS photos were taken, using location data stored <em>inside those photos</em> on your device.</p>
    <p><strong>We do not operate a backend that stores your photos or your location.</strong></p>

    <h2>3. Information we do NOT collect</h2>
    <p>CATMAN STUDIO does <strong>not</strong> collect, store on our servers, or receive:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Your name, email address, or contact details (unless you email us voluntarily)</li>
      <li>Real-time or historical GPS tracks</li>
      <li>Your photos or image files</li>
      <li>Device identifiers for advertising</li>
      <li>Usage analytics tied to your identity</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>4. Data that stays on your device</h2>
    <p>All of the following remain under your control on your phone or tablet:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Photos you capture and save to your gallery</li>
      <li>GPS coordinates embedded in photo metadata (EXIF) when you take a picture with location enabled</li>
      <li>In-app settings (language, logo, notes, preferences)</li>
      <li>Photos displayed in the in-app gallery and on the map</li>
    </ul>
    <p>You can delete the app at any time. Photos already saved in your device gallery remain until you delete them in your gallery app.</p>

    <h2>5. Permissions and why they are needed</h2>
    <h3>Camera</h3>
    <p>To take photos. Images are saved locally; we do not receive them.</p>
    <h3>Location (while you use the app)</h3>
    <p>To write coordinates into the photo you are taking and to show your position when relevant. We do not send your live location to CATMAN STUDIO.</p>
    <h3>Photos / media (Android 11+)</h3>
    <p>To show your CamGPS photos in the gallery and on the map. Access is local on your device.</p>
    <h3>Media location (Android 10+)</h3>
    <p>To read GPS metadata inside your photos for the map feature. Still processed on your device; not sent to CATMAN STUDIO.</p>
    <h3>Internet</h3>
    <p>Used only when you open the <strong>map screen</strong>, to load map tiles through Google Maps. CATMAN STUDIO does not receive the content of your photos through that service.</p>

    <h2>6. Third-party services</h2>
    <h3>Google Play</h3>
    <p>Downloading or updating the app through Google Play is subject to Google’s own policies.</p>
    <h3>Google Maps</h3>
    <p>If you use the in-app photo map, map display is provided by <strong>Google Maps</strong> (Google LLC). Google may process technical data needed to show the map (for example, IP address, device information) according to <a href="https://policies.google.com/privacy" rel="noopener">Google’s Privacy Policy</a>. CATMAN STUDIO does not receive your photos from Google Maps.</p>
    <p>Other parts of CamGPS (camera, gallery, saving photos) work without internet.</p>

    <h2>7. Children</h2>
    <p>CamGPS is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.</p>

    <h2>8. Security</h2>
    <p>Because we do not collect your personal data on our servers, there is no CATMAN STUDIO database of your photos or locations to breach. Protect your device with a screen lock and keep your operating system updated.</p>

    <h2>9. Changes</h2>
    <p>We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.</p>

    <h2>10. Your rights</h2>
    <p>Because we do not hold your personal data on our systems, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on a server. You control all app data on your device.</p>
